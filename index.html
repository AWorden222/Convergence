How to Play
🌐

Create or join a room

One player creates a room and shares the 4-letter code. The other player enters it to join.

🃏

Each player gets a private card

Your card shows three words — Easy, Medium, and Hard. Only you can see your card.

🤝

Agree on difficulty

Talk it over with your partner and agree on Easy, Medium, or Hard — without saying your actual words aloud. Player 1 confirms the choice.

🧠

Submit connecting words

Each player independently types one word that bridges the active pair. Both answers are revealed at the same time.

🎯

Converge!

If your words match — you've converged! If not, your two submitted words become the new active pair. Keep going until you match.

🏆

Scoring

Easy: 3 pts × attempts  ·  Medium: 2 pts × attempts  ·  Hard: 1 pt × attempts. Lower is better!
